# Nuke Oil

A Factorio mod that allows you to destroy oil patches using atomic bombs.

## Description

Tired of oil patches in inconvenient locations? This mod allows you to permanently remove oil patches from the map by nuking them with atomic bombs. When an atomic bomb explodes near oil resources, they will be destroyed and removed from the map.

## Usage

1. Research and craft atomic bombs as usual
2. Launch an atomic bomb at an oil field you want to remove
3. Watch as the oil patches are destroyed in the blast!
4. The game will display a message showing how many oil resources were destroyed

## Compatibility

- Factorio version: 2.0+
- Should be compatible with most other mods

## Notes

- This permanently removes oil patches - they will not respawn
- Make sure you really want to remove the oil before nuking!
- The mod destroys oil patches in range of 12 tiles
